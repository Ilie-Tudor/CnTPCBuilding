About us

In simpler terms we are enthusiasts. We've allways loved building and tweaking everything in our own systems. From squeezing the last bit of performance the PC has, to customizing the tiniest details, we did it all and now we're doing it for YOU also! We believe computers are pieces of art. Everybody should.


Our mission is to make affordable, good looking and accessible PCs for everyone, regardless of age, ocupation, use case or need!


We offer a wide variety of building services done by passionate people with an affinity for gaming and creativity alike. With the dedication and hard work we are able to provide, we can tailor the right machine for your needs. Moreover, if you have a modding project or simply need a partner to assist you on your first, second or even 100th PC, don't hesitate to contact us. And stay tunned!
